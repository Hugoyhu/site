Hugo Hu


Hi! I'm Hugo. I'm a sophomore who loves PCB design, mail, and photography.

I'm currently a part-time intern for Hack Club working on Blot, a generative art plotting machine. Previously, I've designed hardware for Sprig, helped organize a 42-hour hackathon in SF and worked as Mail Team Coordinator for over two years, working with Intelligent Mail and many carriers domestic and international.

In my free time, I like to design fun PCBs (you can find some on my projects page), taking photos, and participating in my school's math team.
