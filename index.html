Hello!

I'm Hugo. I'm currently a sophomore at Stuyvesant High School, and I'm interested in hardware engineering, logistics and photography.

I'm currently an intern for Hack Club working on PCB design and procurement for Blot, a programmable drawing machine for generative art. Previously, I worked with hardware engineering for Sprig, a game console that provided a platform for beginners with little-to-no experience in coding to develop games in a web editor​, and helped organize Assemble​, the first major in-person hackathon since COVID. 

For more than two years, I've been Mail Team Coordinator for Hack Club, where I took on a plethora of challenges such as lowering shipping costs for domestic and especially international outbound parcels, improving customs clearance and delivery abroad, and integrated modern postal technologies like Intelligent Mail.

While I do hardware design for work, I also greatly enjoy making fun personal projects that interest me in my free time. I'm currently working on building a IMX6 based board that runs linux.
